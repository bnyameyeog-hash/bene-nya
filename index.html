Mathematics Highlight

Third Isomorphism Theorem

Let $G$ be a group and let $N \subseteq K \subseteq G$ be normal subgroups of $G$. Then \[ \frac{G/N}{K/N} \cong G/K. \] This result is known as the Third Isomorphism Theorem.
